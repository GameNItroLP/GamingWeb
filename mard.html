Mard is an indie horror game developed and published by Black Curtain Studio. Your job is to be a detective and you decide to
take time off work to go out to your dad’s log cabin in the wilderness. Your character drives to the cabin with his trusty dog Speck,
a German Shorthaired Pointer, who will accompany you throughout your journey.
When you arrive at the cabin you immediately realize that something has gone terribly wrong.
Your father is missing. In an effort to find out what happened to your father you search for clues and evidence around the house.
The evidence will lead you and your dog Speck onto a scary adventure.
In order to survive and find your father your character must brave the elements and take advantage of the
resources, he has around him. Nature can be dangerous so don’t get lost in the forest, and don’t wander out
of the cabin at night! Speck can help you get around obstacles and avoid enemies along the way.
All in an effort to discover the mystery of what happened to your father.
